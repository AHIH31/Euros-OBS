Accounts Types Information

Savings Accounts


A savings account is a secure and rewarding way to achieve your financial goals. It is a specialized bank account that allows you to save and grow your money over time. With a savings account, you can deposit your funds and watch them accumulate through competitive interest rates. It's an ideal option for building an emergency fund, saving for a dream vacation, or planning for the future. Our online banking platform makes it easy for you to manage your savings account from the comfort of your home or on the go. Track your balance, deposit or withdraw funds, set savings goals, and monitor your progress effortlessly. We prioritize the security of your savings, employing advanced encryption and multi-factor authentication measures to safeguard your funds. Start your journey towards financial success with our user-friendly online banking system and maximize the benefits of a savings account today.

Current Accounts


A current account is a versatile financial tool that empowers you to manage your day-to-day transactions effortlessly. It is designed for your everyday banking needs, offering a convenient way to deposit, withdraw, and access your funds whenever required. With a current account, you can seamlessly handle various financial activities such as paying bills, making purchases, and transferring money to friends and family. Our online banking system ensures that you have full control over your current account, allowing you to monitor your balance, track your transactions, and review your transaction history at your convenience. We prioritize the security of your account, implementing robust measures to protect your data and transactions. With our user-friendly interface and intuitive features, you can navigate your current account seamlessly and manage your finances efficiently. Experience the convenience and flexibility of a current account with our online banking platform today and simplify your financial journey.
